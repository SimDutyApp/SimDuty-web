Overview

This page explains how to read core Home metrics before and during each trip.

Estimated time: 3 minutes Purpose: operational visibility Output: safer trip decisions

Key indicators

  • Safety & Performance Score
  • Net Profit and Trip Profit
  • Active Trip (cargo and ETA)
  • Truck Health
  • Drive Limit and Shift Left

Recommended workflow

  1. Confirm current duty state (ON/OFF DUTY) before dispatch.
  2. Review available driving and shift limits before departure.
  3. Track profitability and vehicle condition throughout the trip.

Validation checkpoint

  • Duty status is correct before departure.
  • Drive and shift limits are reviewed.
  • Trip and profit indicators update during operation.

Quick support

  • If trip data looks stale, confirm SDK/NET status in the top bar.
  • If limits look wrong, confirm active policy and current duty state.
  • If cards do not refresh, restart SimDuty before reopening the game.
